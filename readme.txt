Introduction
Tasty Recipes is a platform where food enthusiasts can explore a wide variety of recipes. Our goal is to make cooking accessible and fun for everyone. Each recipe includes step-by-step instructions, ingredient lists, cooking times, and photos or videos to guide you through the cooking process.

Features
Extensive Recipe Collection: Browse through a diverse collection of recipes, including appetizers, main courses, desserts, and more.
Step-by-Step Instructions: Each recipe comes with detailed steps and tips to ensure you get the best results.
Photos and Videos: Visual aids to help you see how the dishes should look at each stage of preparation.
Search and Filter: Easily find recipes based on ingredients, cuisine, dietary preferences, and difficulty levels.
User Reviews and Ratings: Read feedback from other users to help you choose the best recipes to try.
Save Your Favorites: Create a personal collection of your favorite recipes for quick access.
Getting Started
To start exploring and cooking with Tasty Recipes, simply visit our website Tasty Recipes and create a free account. This will allow you to save your favorite recipes, leave reviews, and more.

How to Use
Browse Recipes: Navigate through different categories or use the search bar to find specific recipes.
View Recipe Details: Click on a recipe to see the full details, including ingredients, cooking instructions, and nutritional information.
Follow Step-by-Step Instructions: Use the provided photos or videos to guide you through the cooking process.
Leave a Review: After trying a recipe, leave a review and rate it to help other users.
Save Your Favorites: Click the save button on any recipe to add it to your personal collection.
Contributing
We welcome contributions from the community! If you have a recipe you'd like to share or improvements you'd like to suggest, please follow these steps:

Fork the repository.
Create a new branch (git checkout -b feature/your-feature).
Commit your changes (git commit -m 'Add your feature').
Push to the branch (git push origin feature/your-feature).
Open a Pull Request.
Please ensure your contributions adhere to our Code of Conduct.
